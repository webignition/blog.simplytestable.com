Our sixth updates newsletter went out today.

Highlights - internal prototype progress:

  • the first prototype showed a test progressing, presenting an overall progress bar and three additional bars showing the number of tests in relation to their state (queued, in progress or completed)

  • the second prototype presented in addition the list of URLs being tested, the tests being performed against them and the state of each test

  • the third prototype added further the dynamic presentation of error counts per test as tests completed

Expect the next in a week from now, September 5 2012.
